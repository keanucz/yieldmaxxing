# Principles

The vision, design principles, and ground rules for CropGuard.

## Vision

A neutral, free, satellite-first fertility platform that any European corn grower — including the under-100-hectare farms the incumbents ignore — can use to know exactly **which** fertilizer to apply **where**, at **what rate**, and **when**. Long-term, expand beyond corn (silage maize, wheat, barley, sunflower) and beyond the EU (Ukraine, Turkey, North Africa, Brazilian smallholders), keeping the same neutrality and zero-cost-of-entry posture.

## Design principles

The non-negotiables that drive every product decision.

- **Neutral by construction.** We don't sell fertilizer, seed, or equipment. The recommendation engine is free to say "switch off CAN" or "skip the branded product" because nothing in our P&L depends on the answer.
- **Variable rate AND variable product.** Every incumbent solves rate. We solve rate *and* product (CAN vs urea vs UAN vs ammonium sulfate; DAP vs MAP; MOP vs SOP) at the zone level. This is the wedge.
- **Free baseline, always.** The core prescription product is free for the farmer, forever. Premium = optional integrations (drones, sensors, multi-farm, advanced exports).
- **Smallholder-first.** Designed for the 60% of EU corn farms under 100 ha. Incumbents optimize for the 1% over 1,000 ha; we invert that priority.
- **Fusion-ready, baseline-sufficient.** The system delivers a usable prescription with only the free global baseline (Sentinel-2 + LUCAS + Copernicus + DEM). Every additional source — lab tests, yield maps, drone imagery, IoT probes, N-sensors, phone photos — improves accuracy without being required.
- **Atfarm-bar UX.** 5-minute onboarding, mobile-first, offline-capable, local language (IT, FR, ES, DE, PL, RO from day one), one-screen output, no hardware required.
- **Crop-specific defaults.** The user picks "corn" and the system adapts: growth stages (V4, V6, side-dress, R1), application windows, target rates, common fertilizer mixes.
- **Outputs the farmer can actually use.** ISO-XML / Shapefile for VRA spreaders, simplified manual instructions ("drive faster here, slower there") for farmers without VRA equipment, printable PDFs for the cab without connectivity.
- **Trust through endorsement, not marketing.** Universities, ag extensions, and farmer co-ops carry more weight with this audience than any feature list.

## What this is / what this isn't

**Is:**
- A free, web- and mobile-based variable-rate **and variable-product** fertility platform
- A satellite-first system that fuses Sentinel-2, LUCAS, Copernicus, and DEM data — and ingests optional sources when available
- A zone-level prescription engine that answers *how much, which, and when* for the full NPK stack
- Built for the smallholder EU corn grower (Phase 1: France, Romania, Poland, Hungary, Germany, Italy)
- Neutral — no fertilizer or seed sold

**Isn't:**
- A fertilizer reseller or marketplace
- A general farm-management ERP (no accounting, no payroll, no equipment maintenance)
- An industrial-scale-only product (we don't gate features behind 1,000+ ha)
- A hardware play (no proprietary sensors required)
- A walled garden (we export to ISO-XML / Shapefile so farmers stay in control of their data)
